What is revenue decay detection?+
Revenue decay detection monitors buyer behavior signals — not rep activity — to identify at-risk B2B deals before they fail. Unlike CRM stage changes (logged after the fact), decay detection tracks buyer silence, stakeholder disengagement, and procurement stalls in real time. Bizwinner detects these signals an average of 19 days before your CRM reflects them — giving you the intervention window.
Why does my CRM show a healthy pipeline until it's too late?+
CRMs are rep-reported systems. A rep updates a stage when they decide to — not when the buyer disengages. When your champion goes silent, procurement stalls, or a competitor enters the conversation — none of that is automatically captured. The result is a structural 19-day lag between when a deal starts dying and when your forecast reflects it. This isn't a CRM failure. It's a design limitation Bizwinner fills.
How does Bizwinner help CFOs give the board more reliable guidance?+
CFOs currently rely on CRM data that is rep-reported and structurally lagged. Bizwinner provides an independent, buyer-behavior-based signal layer that validates or contradicts CRM pipeline data in real time. This gives CFOs a second opinion on forecast quality before committing to board guidance — reducing the risk of a public forecast miss.
Does Bizwinner replace our CRM or require IT?+
No on both counts. Bizwinner is a read-only signal layer that sits on top of Salesforce, HubSpot, Outreach, or Gong. It does not write to your CRM, change any workflow, require rep adoption, or need IT for deployment. Your existing stack stays exactly as it is.
How do PE firms and investors use this?+
PE firms use Bizwinner to establish independent pipeline health monitoring across portfolio companies — a signal layer that doesn't depend on management's CRM data. During due diligence, forecast accuracy and pipeline conversion rates directly impact valuation. Bizwinner gives operating partners a verifiable signal layer that improves forecast discipline and surfaces revenue risk early.
Are the numbers on this site from real customers?+
Not yet — and we say so clearly. All projected outcomes come from a virtual pilot: our detection algorithm run against 240 deal patterns from Clari, Gong, and Pavilion benchmark datasets. These are model projections. We will only publish verified numbers once design partners run Bizwinner against live pipelines and consent to sharing results.
